## Procedures and Processes

### Step 1: Initial Contact & Quote
- Call (619) 906-9018 for free estimate
- Company collects information about:
  - Current location and destination address
  - Volume and nature of items being moved
  - Special requirements or considerations
- Estimate provided based on anticipated duration and truck size

### Step 2: Pre-Move Preparation
- Organize belongings for loading
- Clear pathways at origin and destination
- Quick pack totes provided for unboxed items (included in service)
- Protective materials provided by company (no need to purchase separately)

### Step 3: Moving Day Operations
- Professional movers arrive with selected truck
- All equipment pre-loaded on truck
- Movers assess the move and begin loading process

### Step 4: Loading Protocol
- Moving pads deployed to protect furniture and walls
- Shrink wrap used to secure items within truck
- Hand trucks utilized for heavy items and boxes
- Lifting straps used for safe handling of heavy furniture
- Strategic loading to maximize space and prevent damage

### Step 5: Transportation
- Secure all items with shrink wrap and proper securing techniques
- Direct transportation to destination within San Diego County
- Professional driving with care for customer belongings

### Step 6: Unloading & Placement
- Unload truck at destination
- Place items as directed by customer
- Furniture positioning per customer instructions
- Boxes placed in designated rooms
- Moving pads used during unloading to protect property

### Step 7: Completion
- Remove all protective materials
- Final walkthrough with customer
- Payment processed based on actual hours worked
